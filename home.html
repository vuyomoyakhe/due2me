Due2Me

About

Due2Me is a simple yet powerful record-keeping tool designed for individuals who lend money to others for profit. It helps users track loans, manage repayments, and maintain organized financial records without relying on complex software or pen and paper that can easily be misplaced or forgotten.

With Due2Me, lenders can log and update loans anytime, from anywhere, using their smartphones. A built-in reminder system ensures that borrowers are notified when payments are due, making it easier to follow up. The app also allows users to track partial payments, set repayment terms, and generate reports for better financial management.
